# FleshNote Plot Planner

The Plot Planner is a comprehensive, interactive timeline tool designed to help you organize the narrative structure, pacing, and events of your story.

## Core Features

### Infinite Zoomable Timeline
The planner features a horizontally scrolling timeline that can be zoomed in or out. 
- Use the **ZOOM slider** to compress or widen the timeline view. Zooming out allows you to see the entire story path at a glance, while zooming in lets you focus on granular beat-by-beat details.

### Story Blocks (Beats)
Blocks represent individual events or plot points.
- **Types:** Categorize blocks dynamically (e.g., Beat, Reveal, Twist, Climax).
- **Lanes:** Drag and drop blocks horizontally to place them exactly where they occur in the timeline, and vertically into different lanes to separate concurrent events or character arcs.
- **Smart Compression:** If blocks are placed too close together in the same lane, they will automatically "squish" into a compact width and hide their text to prevent UI overlap and visual clutter, expanding back when given space.

### Narrative Arcs
Arcs represent long-running consequences, character developments, or overarching storylines.
- They are rendered below the main timeline rail.
- Arcs have resizable handles at their start and end points to adjust their duration. 
- You can right-click or click an arc to add descriptive text.

### Layering System
To handle complex stories with hidden agendas or subtext, the planner uses layers:
- **Surface Layer:** What the reader or the main characters explicitly know is happening.
- **Shadow Layer:** The underlying mechanics, secret plots, or hidden motivations occurring behind the scenes.
- You can toggle the visibility of the inactive layer to either focus entirely on one aspect or see how both interweave.

### Interactive Drag & Drop
- Dragging blocks and resizing arcs are mapped precisely to the timeline's internal coordinates, allowing for accurate manipulation even when zoomed out or scrolled far to the right. 

### "You Are Here" Indicator
The planner integrates with your chapters. Based on your currently selected chapter and overall chapter count, a marker will drop down on the timeline indicating approximately where you currently are in the narrative flow.
